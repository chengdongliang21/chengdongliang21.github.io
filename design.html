設計理念 / Design Concept

哈爾濱歷史文化壁畫/Harbin Historical Culture Murals：
第一張壁畫聚焦哈爾濱經典歷史文化歷史照片的視覺呈現，以中央大街、索菲亞教堂、俄式建築等標誌性元素為核心，融入冰雪文化、地域民俗符號，通過場景化構圖展現城市的歷史底蘊與異域風情。畫面弱化商業屬性，側重營造「沉浸式歷史氛圍」，讓顧客在用餐時直觀感受哈爾濱的城市記憶，同時通過色調與餐廳空間融合，成為傳遞地域文化的裝飾載體。顧客反饋：「照片牆能感受到老哈爾濱的氣息」。

The first mural focuses on the visual presentation of Harbin's classic historical culture, with iconic elements such as Central Street, Sophia Church, and Russian-style architecture at its core, incorporating ice and snow culture and regional folk symbols. Through scenographic composition, it showcases the city's historical heritage and exotic charm. The design downplays commercial attributes, emphasizing the creation of an "immersive historical atmosphere" that allows diners to directly experience Harbin's urban memory while dining. The color scheme integrates with the restaurant space, serving as a decorative medium for conveying regional culture. Customer feedback: "The photo wall evokes the essence of old Harbin."

第二張壁畫以「哈爾濱地域文化+福上牛品牌」為雙重內核，在呈現城市歷史元素的基礎上，巧妙融入「牛骨頭小盤菜」的美食關聯——例如通過老哈爾濱市集裡的牛骨攤位、傳統烹飪場景等細節，呼應「一城一味」的概念，將城市歷史與餐廳主打美食綁定。畫面既保留歷史厚重感，又通過「福上牛」品牌符號的隱性植入（如攤位幌子、餐具紋飾），實現「城市記憶」與「美食體驗」的自然銜接，強化顧客對「哈爾濱味道在福上牛」的認知。

The second mural takes "Harbin regional culture + Fushang Niu brand" as its dual core. While presenting urban historical elements, it cleverly incorporates associations with the "beef bone small plate dishes" - such as through details like beef bone stalls in old Harbin markets and traditional cooking scenes, echoing the concept of "one city, one flavor," binding the city's history with the restaurant's signature dishes. The design preserves historical gravitas while subtly embedding "Fushang Niu" brand symbols (like stall signs and utensil patterns), achieving a natural connection between "urban memory" and "culinary experience," reinforcing customers' perception of "Harbin flavors at Fushang Niu."

食材宣傳海報/Ingredient Promotion Posters：

第一張裝飾宣傳海報：以「草原溜達身強壯」為核心賣點，聚焦餐廳所用牛食材的來源與品質。畫面可呈現草原遼闊風光，點綴健碩的溜達牛群，搭配「福上牛牛骨頭小盤菜」品牌名，直觀傳遞食材來自草原散養、體格強健的特點，凸顯食材原生態與優質，吸引注重食材品質的顧客

1. The first decorative promotional poster: With "free-range grazing makes strong cattle" as the core selling point, it focuses on the source and quality of the beef ingredients used in the restaurant. The image presents vast grassland scenery dotted with robust free-range cattle, paired with the "Fushang Niu Beef Bone Small Plate Dishes" brand name, directly conveying the characteristics of pasture-raised, physically strong ingredients, highlighting their natural and premium quality to attract customers who value ingredient quality.

第二張裝飾宣傳海報：緊扣「現燉現炒 菜整挺好」，展現食材的新鮮與烹飪的匠心。通過描繪現處理牛骨頭、現燉煮或現炒制的動態場景，搭配冒著熱氣的菜品特寫，突出「現做」的新鮮質感，傳遞餐廳對食材處理和烹飪過程的用心，讓顧客感受到菜品的地道滋味與煙火氣。

2. The second decorative promotional poster: Closely tied to "freshly stewed, freshly stir-fried, dishes done right," it showcases the freshness of ingredients and the craftsmanship of cooking. By depicting dynamic scenes of freshly processed beef bones, current stewing or stir-frying, paired with steaming dish close-ups, it emphasizes the "made fresh" quality, conveying the restaurant's dedication to ingredient handling and cooking processes, allowing customers to experience the authentic flavors and lively atmosphere of the dishes.

第三張裝飾宣傳海報：圍繞「江河好味道 鮮掉你眉毛」展開，強化食材帶來的鮮美口感。結合哈爾濱江河元素，畫面可融入江河風光，搭配牛骨頭菜品的誘人色澤，用生動的視覺效果和文案呼應「鮮掉眉毛」的味覺體驗，激發顧客的食慾，吸引追求鮮美口感的食客。

3. The third decorative promotional poster: Centered on "river's good flavor, freshness that astonishes," it enhances the delicious taste brought by the ingredients. Incorporating Harbin's river elements, the image blends river scenery with the appealing colors of beef bone dishes, using vivid visual effects and copy to echo the "astonishingly fresh" taste experience, stimulating customers' appetite and attracting diners who pursue exquisite flavors.

設計流程 / Design Process

1、素材收集與梳理

整理哈爾濱歷史文化影像資料（建築、民俗等）及草原牛群、烹飪場景、江河風光等食材相關素材，確保內容貼合主題。

Collected and organized Harbin's historical and cultural visual materials (architecture, folk customs, etc.) and ingredient-related materials such as grassland cattle, cooking scenes, and river landscapes to ensure content relevance to the theme.

2、初稿繪製

使用sketchbook手繪工具，勾勒哈爾濱歷史文化壁畫的場景輪廓（如俄式建築與冰雪元素結合）及食材海報的主體形象（草原牛、現燉炒鍋、鮮味示意圖形），形成初步視覺框架。

Used Sketchbook hand-drawing tools to outline scene compositions for Harbin historical murals (such as combining Russian architecture with ice-snow elements) and main visuals for ingredient posters (grassland cattle, stewing woks, freshness indication graphics), establishing preliminary visual frameworks.

3、矢量化與排版

透過Illustrator將手繪初稿轉化為矢量圖形，對壁畫進行歷史文化元素的細節填充（如建築紋飾、民俗符號），對海報進行文字與圖形排版（突出「福上牛牛骨頭小盤菜」品牌名及食材賣點文案）。

Used Illustrator to convert hand-drawn drafts into vector graphics, filling in details of historical and cultural elements for murals (such as architectural ornaments and folk symbols), and arranging typography and graphics for posters (highlighting the "Fushang Niu Beef Bone Small Plate Dishes" brand name and ingredient selling points copy).

4、調色與合成

運用photoshop進行調色，壁畫採用厚重而富有歷史感的色調（如暖棕、米白搭配俄式藍），海報採用鮮活明快的色調（如草原綠、食材本味的暖黃、江河藍），增強視覺吸引力，盡力表現出「風吹草低見牛羊」的草原蒼茫，突出原生食材的品質；最終合成輸出，確保5個作品風格統一且符合餐廳裝飾需求。

Used Photoshop for color grading: murals employed rich, historically evocative tones (like warm browns, off-whites paired with Russian blues), while posters used vibrant, lively tones (like grassland greens, ingredient-warm yellows, river blues) to enhance visual appeal, striving to express the vast "wind-swept grasslands revealing cattle" scenery and highlight the quality of natural ingredients; final compositing ensured stylistic consistency across all five works while meeting restaurant decoration requirements.

項目 2：凱里亞德酒店海報設計
Project 2: Kyriad Hotel Promotional Poster Design

項目簡介 / Project Introduction

數量：1个/套

Quantity: 1 piece/set

類型：宣傳海報

Type: Promotional Poster

技術工具：平面設計軟體：Adobe illustrator / 調色軟體：Adobe photoshop

設計理念 / Design Concept

聚焦凱里亞德酒店「中高端連鎖+樞紐旅居」定位，以模組化版式串聯品牌背書、交通、服務、配套、區位五大核心資訊，透過品牌藍金配色與場景化實拍圖，構建「信任→便利→體驗」的資訊動線，助力酒店在深圳北站商圈精準傳遞「高效旅居解決方案」，強化品牌辨識度與客群吸引力。

Focusing on Kyriad Hotel's "mid-to-high-end chain + hub accommodation" positioning, the design connects five core elements—brand endorsement, transportation, services, amenities, and location—through modular layout. Using brand blue-gold color schemes and contextual real-life photos, it constructs an information flow of "trust → convenience → experience," helping the hotel accurately convey its "efficient accommodation solution" in Shenzhen North Station business district while strengthening brand recognition and customer appeal.

設計流程 / Design Process

1、需求評審

深度對接酒店方，明確三大核心訴求： ① 強化「錦江集團+歐洲連鎖品牌」的背書價值，建立信任感； ② 突出「深圳北站樞紐+多維交通網絡」的區位優勢，吸引中轉/商務客群； ③ 展示「全房型+全配套+特色服務」的體驗亮點，傳遞「中高端旅居」定位。同時調研場景：海報用於線下門店展示、周邊商圈拓客，需兼顧「信息清晰」與「視覺吸引力」，適配快節奏瀏覽習慣。

In-depth communication with hotel management to clarify three core requirements: ① Strengthen the endorsement value of "Jinjiang Group + European chain brand" to build trust; ② Highlight the locational advantage of "Shenzhen North Station hub + multi-dimensional transportation network" to attract transit/business clientele; ③ Showcase experiential highlights of "full room types + complete amenities + special services" to convey "mid-to-high-end accommodation" positioning. Also researched application scenarios: posters for in-store display and nearby business district promotion, requiring balance between "clear information" and "visual appeal" to suit fast-paced browsing habits.

2、主題策劃

結合品牌主張「活在當下，盡享此刻」，提煉「樞紐旁的品質驛居——凱里亞德的深圳提案」主題： - 以「樞紐旁」錨定交通核心優勢，「品質驛居」呼應中高端定位； - 串聯「連鎖品牌（信任）→交通便利（效率）→配套齊全（體驗）→地理優越（潛力）」的價值邏輯，讓「活在當下」的理念具象為「高效+舒適」的旅居解決方案。

Aligned with brand proposition "Live in the moment, enjoy the now," refined the theme "Quality Hub-side Accommodation - Kyriad's Shenzhen Proposal": - "Hub-side" anchors transportation core advantage, "quality accommodation" echoes mid-to-high-end positioning; - Connects value logic of "chain brand (trust) → transportation convenience (efficiency) → complete amenities (experience) → geographical advantage (potential)," materializing the "live in the moment" concept into an "efficient + comfortable" accommodation solution.

3、视觉体系构建

▶ 色彩：沿用品牌標準藍，搭配暖金色點綴，平衡「商務屬性」與「溫馨體驗」；
▶ 字體：標題採用厚重型襯線字體，正文用簡潔無襯線體，通過字重對比強化信息層級；
▶ 版式：採用「模塊分區 + 視覺引導線」設計 —— 頂部品牌信息區、中部核心賣點區、底部區位價值區，用藍金分割線串聯板塊，引導視線流動。

Visual System Construction:
▶ Colors: Maintained brand standard blue with warm gold accents, balancing "business attributes" and "cozy experience";
▶ Typography: Used bold serif fonts for headlines and clean sans-serif for body text, strengthening information hierarchy through font weight contrast;
▶ Layout: Employed "modular zoning + visual guide lines" design—top brand information zone, middle core selling points zone, bottom location value zone, connected by blue-gold dividing lines to guide visual flow.

4、細節策略落地

▶ 數據可視化：將「4 公里 15 分鐘」「2.4 米大床」等核心數據轉化為圖標 + 數字組合，降低理解成本；
▶ 場景故事化：精選客房（溫馨感）、公區（精緻感）、健身房（專業感）實拍圖，通過光影優化 + 氛圍調色（如客房暖光、公區柔光）傳遞「舒適旅居」體驗；；
▶ 品牌滲透：在大堂場景圖中植入「Kyriad」標識，客房細節融入「晚安牛奶」等服務元素，讓品牌理念自然滲透；；
▶ 在在地化呼應：突出「華為 / 富士康園區」「山姆會員店」等深圳在地符號，強化「区位紅利」與「城市適配性」。

Detail Strategy Implementation:
▶ Data Visualization: Transformed core data like "4km 15min" and "2.4m large bed" into icon+number combinations to reduce comprehension cost;
▶ Scenario Storytelling: Selected actual photos of guest rooms (coziness), public areas (refinement), gym (professionalism), conveying "comfortable stay" experience through light/shadow optimization and ambient color grading (e.g., warm lighting for rooms, soft lighting for public areas);
▶ Brand Integration: Embedded "Kyriad" logo in lobby scenes, incorporated service elements like "goodnight milk" in room details for natural brand concept permeation;
▶ Localization: Highlighted Shenzhen landmarks like "Huawei/Foxconn campuses" and "Sam's Club" to strengthen "location benefits" and "city compatibility."

  • 需求評審：負責與酒店方深度對接，明確核心訴求與應用場景
  • 主題策劃：負責提煉設計主題與價值邏輯
  • 視覺設計：負責構建完整的視覺體系並執行細節設計
  • 品牌整合：負責確保所有元素符合品牌調性與定位

  • Requirements Review: Responsible for in-depth communication with hotel to clarify core needs and application scenarios
  • Theme Planning: Responsible for refining design theme and value logic
  • Visual Design: Responsible for constructing complete visual system and executing detail design
  • Brand Integration: Responsible for ensuring all elements align with brand tone and positioning

項目3：萬佳優鮮折扣批發超市品牌海報設計
Project 3: Wanjia Youxian Discount Wholesale Supermarket Brand Poster

查看高清設計圖 / View HD Design

項目簡介 / Project Introduction

數量：1个/套

Quantity: 1 piece/set

類型：商業超市牌匾

Type: Commercial Supermarket Signage

技術工具：手繪工具：sketchbook / 平面設計軟體：Adobe Illustrator / 調色軟體：Adobe Photoshop

Tools: Sketchbook / Adobe Illustrator / Adobe Photoshop

設計理念 / Design Concept

和老板娘閒聊時，她六歲的兒子正蹲在貨架旁數罐頭——這是萬佳優鮮最日常的畫面：老板娘繫着磨白的圍裙理貨，孩子抱着零食罐在腳邊打轉，熟客總笑說「像逛親戚家倉庫」。設計時，我決定把這份「家庭式煙火」搬上畫面：臨摹老板娘和孩子的真實神態——她眼角笑出的細紋藏着守店的踏實，孩子拽着圍裙角的依賴裡，是無數個陪媽媽看店的午後。炸裂的黃色背景裡，粗黑字體像老板娘拍着胸脯喊「誰敢跟我比價格」，但母子親昵的姿態，又把「一件也是批發價」的硬氣，柔化成「自家店不賺你差價」的懇切。從捕捉老板娘帶娃守店的生活切片，到手繪定格這份親昵，再讓犀利的價格主張和柔軟的親子互動碰撞——這張海報最終成了一面鏡子：照見家庭小店的真誠，也讓「實惠」二字，帶着老板娘圍裙上的溫度。

While chatting with the owner, her six-year-old son was squatting by the shelves counting cans—this was Wanjia Youxian's most ordinary scene: the owner wearing a faded apron organizing goods, her child spinning around with snack cans at her feet, regulars often joking it "feels like visiting a relative's warehouse." For the design, I decided to bring this "family-style daily life" onto the canvas: capturing the owner and child's authentic expressions—the owner's smile lines at her eyes holding the steadfastness of shopkeeping, the child's dependence shown by tugging her apron corner, embodying countless afternoons spent keeping mom company. Against a vibrant yellow background, bold black fonts resemble the owner thumping her chest declaring "Who dares compare prices with me?", while the mother-child intimacy softens the bold "wholesale prices even for single items" into the earnestness of "our shop won't overcharge you." From capturing this slice of shopkeeping-with-child life to freeze-framing this intimacy through hand-drawing, then colliding sharp price propositions with tender parent-child interaction—this poster ultimately became a mirror: reflecting the sincerity of a family-run store, and letting the word "affordable" carry the warmth from the owner's apron.

設計流程 / Design Process

1、需求評審

對接超市方，明確兩大核心訴求：
① 突出「一件也是批發價」的價格競爭力，吸引社區家庭客群；
② 塑造「親民、活力」的品牌人格，區別於傳統批發業態。同時明確應用場景：門店招牌、商圈導視，需適配「遠距離視覺抓取」與「品牌記憶點沉澱」。

Communicated with supermarket management to clarify two core requirements:
① Highlight price competitiveness of "wholesale prices even for single items" to attract community family customers;
② Shape an "approachable, vibrant" brand personality distinct from traditional wholesale formats. Also defined application scenarios: store signage and business district wayfinding, requiring adaptation for both "long-distance visual capture" and "brand memory point retention."

2、主題策劃

提煉「家庭式實惠：敢比價格的優鮮選擇」主題，以卡通親子形象傳遞「家庭消費」的場景關聯，用「誰敢跟我比價格」的強衝突文案強化價格主張，搭建「視覺親和力+價值衝擊力」的雙維傳播邏輯。

Refined the theme "Family-style Affordability: The Price-daring Youxian Choice," using cartoon parent-child imagery to convey "family consumption" scenario association, with bold conflict copy like "Who dares compare prices with me?" to strengthen price proposition, establishing a dual-dimensional communication logic of "visual approachability + value impact."

3、風格錨定

高飽和黃色背景（搶佔注意力）+粗黑斷裂字體（呼應"折扣"的靈活與"批發"的厚重）+圓潤卡通形象（軟化商業感，拉近與家庭客群距離）。

High-saturation yellow background (attention-grabbing) + bold black fractured fonts (echoing "discount" flexibility and "wholesale" solidity) + rounded cartoon figures (softening commercial feel, bridging distance with family customers).

4、細節落地

字體設計透過筆畫斷裂、傾斜角度強化「活力感」，卡通人物以暖色系腮紅、互動姿態傳遞「陪伴式消費」氛圍，文案分區遵循「品牌名→促銷語→門店資訊」的視覺動線，確保資訊高效觸達。
